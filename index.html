Mint Your Token

Yeah we know that for an NFT project, everyone hopes that it can be free mint. But we are another form of free (the fee you paid for NFT will be returned to you in the form of tokens).
So for the healthy development of our token and the entire ecology, we charge a fee of $2 for each minting, and all revenue will be injected into the pool after the token is released.
我们了解大家都喜欢免费铸造，但为了后续代币的发展，我们暂时收取2USD的费用，所有的铸造收益都会在代币发行后注入到流动性池子中，回馈给社区，持有NFT的用户将会被免费空投代币
